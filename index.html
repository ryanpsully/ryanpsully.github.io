Mentour

Mentour is an iOS application that allows prospective students embark on customized tours after creating connections with Indiana University students on campus. With Mentour acting as an alternative to university group tours, our goal is to facilitate a peer to peer connection that enables prospective students to tailor their own touring experience. To elaborate on functionality, Mentour uses a “matching” format in which prospective students will be able to “swipe” on peer coaches (current IU students) in order to connect with someone who aligns with their interests. After connecting, students can begin messaging their peer coach to learn more about the IU campus, or schedule a tour.

Mentour was built in collbaration of a team and part of my Senior year Capstone project and my first experince building an IOS application. Mentours project booth can be located here Mentour Booth

Langauges and Software used: Swift | Php | SQL | Firebase Authentication | Xcode | Procreate
